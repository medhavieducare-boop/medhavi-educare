🎯Our Primary Destinations

MBBS in Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a premier choice for students due to its high FMGE passing rate and a medical curriculum that is almost identical to the Indian education system.

  • Identical Study Pattern: The syllabus and medical books are the same as in India, making it easier to clear licensing exams like NEXT/NMC.
  • Highest Clinical Exposure: High patient inflow in affiliated hospitals provides students with extensive hands-on practical experience.
  • Cultural & Food Affinity: Similar climate, culture, and food habits ensure that students feel at home without any major lifestyle adjustments.

MBBS in Vietnam

Explore world-class facilities and modern infrastructure in Vietnam.

  • Modern Infrastructure: Universities are equipped with state-of-the-art simulation centers, advanced laboratories, and research facilities.
  • Global Recognition: Degrees are accredited by WHO and NMC, allowing graduates to practice medicine globally after clearing required exams.
  • Affordable Quality: Offers a high standard of living and premium medical education at a significantly lower cost than private colleges in India.

MBBS in Russia

Russia has long been a top destination for international medical students, offering a vast network of state-funded universities recognized globally.

  • World-Ranked Universities: Many institutions are listed in the WHO and UNESCO directories, offering high-standard education.
  • Subsidized Tuition Fees: Affordable cost of education due to government subsidies for international students.
  • Dual Medium of Instruction: Availability of full English-medium programs for the entire duration of the course.

MBBS in Georgia

Georgia is becoming a favorite for its European lifestyle and high-quality medical curriculum that focuses heavily on practical clinical exposure.

  • European Education Standards: Universities follow the European Credit Transfer System (ECTS) for global mobility.
  • Safety and Hospitality: Consistently ranked as one of the safest countries in the world for international students.
  • No Entrance Exams: Direct admission based on high school performance without the need for IELTS or TOEFL.

MBBS in Kyrgyzstan

Kyrgyzstan offers one of the most cost-effective paths to an MBBS degree, making it highly accessible for students prioritizing a budget-friendly education.

  • Lowest Cost of Living: Extremely affordable hostel and food expenses compared to other Asian or European countries.
  • Focus on MCI/NMC Screening: Curriculum is specifically designed to help students clear licensing exams in their home countries.
  • Direct Flight Connectivity: Easy and frequent travel options between major Indian cities and Bishkek.

MBBS in Uzbekistan

Uzbekistan provides a blend of historical prestige and modern medical advancements, with universities that have invested heavily in infrastructure recently.

  • Advanced Infrastructure: Recently renovated campuses with modern simulation centers and smart classrooms.
  • Cultural Affinity: Similar climate and cultural values, making it very easy for South Asian students to adapt.
  • Government-Backed Quality: Strict quality control by the Ministry of Health ensures high academic standards across all medical institutes.
